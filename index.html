INTRODUCING

AirPods Max

THE ULTIMATE PERSONAL
LISTENING EXPERIENCE IS HERE.

Apple's AirPods Max headphones combines superb audio performance, superior noise cancellation, and a slew of helpful functions in an incredibly stunning appearance. A perfect balance of exhilarating high-fidelity audio and the effortless magic of AirPods.

FEATURES

Canopy

Made from a breathable knit mesh, distributing weight to reduce onhead pressure

Stainless Steel Frame

Wrapped with a soft-to-the-touch material for a remarkable combination of strength, flexibility, and comfort.

Telescoping Arms

Smoothly extend and stay where you set them, for a consistent fit and seal.

Custom-Designed Mesh Textile

Wraps the ear cushions to provide pillow-like softness while listening.

Anodized Aluminum Cups

Allows each cup to rotate independently and balance pressure.

Ear Cushion

Gently create an immersive seal that is the foundation of incredible sound.

Digital Crown

lets you precisely control volume, skip between tracks, answer phone calls, and activate Siri.
